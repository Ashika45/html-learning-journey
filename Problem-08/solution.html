Tribute to Rashmika Mandanna

Rashmika Mandanna is a popular Indian actress known for her work in Telugu and Kannada films. She has gained immense popularity for her charming screen presence and versatile acting skills.

Early Life and Career

Rashmika Mandanna was born on April 5, 1996, in Virajpet, Karnataka, India. She made her acting debut in the Kannada film "Kirik Party" in 2016, which was a huge success. Her performance in the film earned her several awards and recognition.

Notable films

  • Kirik Party (2016)
  • Andhadhun (2020)
  • Pushpa: The Rise (2021)
  • Geetha Govindam (2018)

Awards and Recognition

  1. Filmfare Award for Best Actress - Kannada (2016) for "Kirik Party"
  2. SIIMA Award for Best Actress - Telugu (2018) for "Geetha Govindam"
  3. SIIMA Award for Best Actress - Telugu (2021) for "Pushpa: The Rise"
  4. Filmfare Award for Best Actress - Telugu (2021) for "Pushpa: The Rise"
